River Cleanup and Conservation Day

Join us for a comprehensive river cleanup and conservation education day at the Green Valley River. This event aims to remove trash and invasive species while learning about the importance of our local watershed ecosystem.

We'll provide all necessary equipment including gloves, trash bags, and tools. Bring water, sunscreen, and wear comfortable clothing suitable for outdoor work. Lunch will be provided for all volunteers.

In addition to the cleanup, we'll have environmental experts conducting short workshops on water conservation, identifying native plants, and sustainable practices to protect our rivers.
